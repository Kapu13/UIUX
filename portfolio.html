Cinema App

The app helps user to book movie ticket without the need to go to cinema itself. The target users are the people who want to watch movie in cinema halls.

The Problem

In cinema halls to watch a movie we have to wait in queue to get the movie ticket. To avoid this problem I have designed a Cinema app.

The Goal

My goal of the project is that a user can book a movie ticket through mobile.

Pain Points

1

Time management

2

Waiting in queue

3

No movie ticket upon arrival at the cinema hall
